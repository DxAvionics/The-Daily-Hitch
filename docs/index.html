The same drones that we celebrate for "turning the tide" in Ukraine today will be sold to the highest bidder tomorrow, and yet we discuss this arms race with the breezy detachment of sports commentators, as if the proliferation of autonomous killing machines were merely another market opportunity rather than the wholesale privatization of death itself.
